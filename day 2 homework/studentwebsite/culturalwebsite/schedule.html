Cultural Program Schedule

  1. Opening Ceremony
  2. Traditional Dance Performance
  3. Group Singing Competition
  4. Drama Performance
  5. Prize Distribution and Closing Ceremony

Opening Ceremony: Welcome speech and inauguration of the program.

Traditional Dance Performance: Students present cultural dances from different regions.

Group Singing Competition: Musical performances by student groups.

Drama Performance: A stage play prepared by students.

Prize Distribution and Closing Ceremony: Awards are presented to winners and participants.
