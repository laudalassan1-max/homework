🎉 Welcome to Sports Week 2026!

Welcome to the Annual Sports Week of our college. This exciting event brings together students from different departments to compete, collaborate, and celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship. Sports Week is not just about winning games—it is about teamwork, dedication, discipline, and creating unforgettable memories.

🌟 Why Sports Week?

Sports Week encourages students to stay active, build confidence, and develop important life skills. It provides an opportunity to showcase athletic talent, make new friends, and represent your department with pride.
